About

Hi, I'm John Baldwin! This is my art and design portfolio where I showcase some of my favorite examples of my work. I love painting, drawing, and photography - really just about anything that has to do with art and creativity. When I am not creating art of some form or fashion, I enjoy learning new things, reading, listening to music, nature, watching sports (especially Clemson and Chicago athletics), hiking, camping, and fishing, and so much more. I try to stay active and keep my hobbies diverse. I love spendning time with my wife and children. Thank you for visiting my page, I hope you enjoy!
